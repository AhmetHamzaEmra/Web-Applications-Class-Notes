Noah's Ark Petting Zoo

Located in Southwick, Massachiset

Come visit Noah's Ark Petiing Zoo. Ride ponies, have a sleepover in the hay loft or wander freely among the animals in our petting zoo. the petting zoo includes rabbits, ducks, pigs, llamas, sheep, pymy goats, miniature donkey and miniature horses

Our barn provides a large heated party room with picnic tables, grill, refrigerator, and restrooms. We can provide you with souvenir hats and pictures and will be glad to suggest a variety of games.
