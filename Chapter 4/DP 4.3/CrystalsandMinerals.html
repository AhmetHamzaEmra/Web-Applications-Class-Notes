Green Apophyllite

Price: $26.00

Size: 10.50 x 7.00 x 6.00 cm

Description: Scolecite and Green Apophyllite from the basalt quarry of Uran, Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra State, India. A very nice, large spray of needles which are semi translucent, grading to quite glassy at the tips. A smaller secondary spray bisects from a perpendicular angle at the bottom and merges with a nice large elongate cubic green apophyllite crystal. The piece has been trimmed at a couple of places at the base, allowing it to stand up and present in a lovely fashion. These scolecite sprays always remind me of a bursting wave that is frozen, as if in a photograph. Stylized and artistic, this combination piece is a beautiful expression from the natural world

Bismuth

Price: $9.95

Size: 3.30 x 1.00 x 4.30 cm

Description: Bismuth Crystal, lab grown in Germany. Since bismuth rarely crystallizes in nature, crystals are slow-cooled out of a melt and acquire the wonderful stacked cubic hopper crystal form. But they are just gray in color, so they are then coated with a titanium vapor to give the metallic rainbow finish. The mathematical perfection, coupled with the striking coloration makes these crystals just about irresistable.
